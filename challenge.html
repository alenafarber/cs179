Manhattan Half Marathon

Back
  • Challenges
  • Alena

This is a challenge that involves running for miles and miles and miles. This workout is a 6 mile run that will take you through scenic Manhattan, along the route of the world famous Manhattan Half Marathon. Start at Central Park, run through Times Square, and cross the finish line at Wall Street. This is a cardio workout that will exercise your legs, arms, and back.
