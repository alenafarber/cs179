  • Land on the middle of your foot and roll through to your toes to avoid stressing your calves or shins.

FitFeats

This challenge has three parts. Check out instructional videos for all three sections here:

How to do a push up:

To decrease difficulty, modify by resting on your knees, not your toes. To increase difficulty, rest your feet on a surface a few inches off the ground.

How to do a chair dip:

This exercise can be done with a sturdy chair, stool, step, bench or any other flat, elevated surface that is about 2 feet off the ground

How to do a bicep curl:

This exercise can be done using any heavy object you find at home, including water bottles, books, bottles of laundry detergent, etc.

IMPORTANT: If you feel discomfort or pain at any time, stop the workout to avoid injury!
